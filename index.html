About

Finance professional
in wealth & investment

I am an Italian finance professional based between London, Verona and Milan, graduated with Distinction from an MSc in Banking & Finance at Queen Mary University of London. My academic foundation was built at Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore in Milan, where I studied Economics & Management, covering financial accounting, corporate law, and taxation. During my BSc, I completed a summer programme in Finance & Financial Markets at King's College London, gaining early exposure to M&A valuation, corporate finance, and behavioural finance. At Queen Mary, I further developed this into applied expertise across wealth management, portfolio construction, and transaction analysis.

My professional path began with a Tax & Accounting internship in Italy, working on financial statements and compliance for SME clients, which gave me a strong understanding of the fiscal dimension of capital. I then joined Fideuram – Intesa Sanpaolo, supporting a Senior Banker in advising HNW clients across asset allocation, investment proposals, and tax-efficient structuring tools. Alongside my studies, I worked as a Lifeguard, developing discipline, leadership, and composure under pressure.

Alongside my academic and professional development, I have consistently engaged in high-impact extracurricular activities. As a member of the UCFA Finance Society, I contributed to macro research and equity analysis. At Queen Mary, I ranked Top 5 as an Equity Analyst within QUMMIF, the university’s student-managed investment fund, contributing to a live £40,000 portfolio. I also ranked Top 10 globally in the UBS & AmplifyME Banking Academy. As Head of Sponsorships at the United Italian Societies, a network of 11,000+ students, I lead institutional partnerships and commercial strategy.

I combine strong analytical foundations with practical exposure to high-net-worth advisory, aiming to build a career in wealth management and cross-border private client strategy.
