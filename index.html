I am an Italian finance professional based in London, completing my MSc in Banking & Finance at Queen Mary University of London, where I am on track for a Distinction. My academic path began at Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore in Milan, where I read Economics & Management.

My professional experience spans private banking at Fideuram – Intesa Sanpaolo, where I supported portfolio advisory for UHNW clients under MiFID II frameworks, and live equity research through QUMMIF, managing a real portfolio and ranking Top-5 as an Equity Analyst.

I served as Head of Sponsorships at the United Italian Societies in London. I combine rigorous quantitative analysis (DCF, LBO) with a focus on institutional capital stewardship and client cross-border complexity.
