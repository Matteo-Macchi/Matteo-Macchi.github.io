About
       

Finance professional

          I am an Italian finance professional based in London, completing my MSc in Banking & Finance           at Queen Mary University of London, where I am on track for a Distinction.           My academic path began at Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore in Milan,           where I read Economics & Management.        

       

          My professional experience spans private banking at Fideuram – Intesa Sanpaolo,           where I supported portfolio advisory for UHNW clients under MiFID II frameworks,           and live equity research through QUMMIF, Queen Mary's student-managed investment fund,           where I ranked Top-5 as an Equity Analyst managing a real portfolio.

I combine rigorous quantitative analysis           with a genuine interest in the human dimension of wealth — understanding client goals,           cross-border complexity, and long-term capital stewardship.
